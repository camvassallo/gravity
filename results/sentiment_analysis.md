## Team-by-Team Consensus

### One Seeds

#### Arizona (1 seed, West) — CONSENSUS BULLISH

- 4/10 title picks. "Best team in the country" (Ralph). Only 2 losses all year by combined 7 points.
- Elite kill shot defense (only 2 ten-zero runs allowed all season). Dominant on the glass.
- **Vulnerability** (bur_5): Near-total absence of 3PT shooting -- historically unprecedented. If they fall behind 8-10, no quick catch-up mechanism. "Can they power ball their way back without the three-point shot?"
- **Consensus**: 9/9 sources bullish. Our model agrees (20.7% to win).
- **Basis**: Data-driven

#### Duke (1 seed, East) — BULLISH WITH INJURY CAVEATS

- Cam Boozer is the best player in the country (universal). Defense elite -- 3 layers of rim protection. Scheyer praised as best coach this year.
- **Key concern**: Caleb Foster OUT, Ngongba questionable. Guard play is the weakness -- "not really talented scorers and not really talented table setters" (bur_5). Multiple sources say injury impact is overblown (Evan Miya: only 1-1.5 pts model adjustment).
- **Disagreement**: Ralph picks St. John's over them in Sweet 16. Sweeney/weave hosts say the draw is tough. Brennan/Frazier still pick them to Final Four.
- **Basis**: Mixed data + vibes

#### Michigan (1 seed, Midwest) — CONSENSUS BULLISH / BRACKET VALUE DARLING

- 4/10 title picks. "Easiest Final Four pick" (Evan Miya, 64% simulation probability). Best bracket value -- only 14% of public brackets pick them despite 18-27% model odds.
- Elite at the rim (60% FG vs opponents' 45%). Got the weakest draw.
- **Vulnerability** (bur_5): Half-court offense bogs down. One-on-one play without elite shot creators. Could score 28 in a first half.
- **Consensus**: Universal Final Four pick across nearly all sources. Our model: 24.5%.
- **Basis**: Data-driven (bracket value play)

#### Florida (1 seed, South) — BEARISH RELATIVE TO SEED

- Only 1 title pick. Multiple sources explicitly decline to pick them out of their own region.
- Got a brutal draw (Houston in Houston for Elite 8, Vanderbilt rematch in Sweet 16). Vanderbilt beat them by 17 in SEC tournament.
- Guard play (Fland, Lee) inconsistent. Turnovers are the real killer, not missed shots.
- **Saving grace**: Elite offensive rebounding (Shinu). Front court is still dominant.
- **Consensus**: 6/9 sources bearish or pick against them. Our model: 5.6% -- gap between seed and reality.
- **Basis**: Mixed -- data supports front court dominance, but vibes/recent results drive the fade

### Two Seeds

#### Houston (2 seed, South) — BULLISH

- Playing in Houston (quasi-home games through Elite 8). Shot volume index: 18/19 similar teams historically made Sweet 16. 3 returning starters from title game.
- **Concern** (bur_5): Excessive mid-range reliance. Shot quality worse than last year. Not as "Houston-like" as typical Houston teams.
- **Consensus**: Most pick them to Elite 8+. Two sources pick them over Florida. Our model: 4.3%.
- **Basis**: Data-driven

#### Iowa State (2 seed, Midwest) — POSITIVE BUT STREAKY

- Evolved offensively under Otzelberger. Big 12 tournament run was electric. 4th best at going on kill shots.
- **Concern**: Gives up 16 kill shots this season -- "not super safe with a lead." Streaky team.
- **Consensus**: Most have Sweet 16, some Elite 8. Several pick Michigan over them. Our model: 2.2%.
- **Basis**: Data-driven

#### Purdue (2 seed, West) — POLARIZING (biggest disagreement across sources)

- **Bull case** (Evan Miya, bur_3 Will): #1 offense nationally. Won Big Ten tournament. Last 4 champs had top-3 offense. 27% Final Four probability vs 15% public -- "almost double the odds."
- **Bear case** (bur_1 Winnick): "Where have you been for the last three months? This team's not that good and they don't play defense. Four days do not define a season."
- **Consensus**: Sharp split. Our model: 4.8% -- higher than most pundits expect.
- **Basis**: Data vs data disagreement -- both sides cite stats

#### UConn (2 seed, East) — BEARISH

- Universal skepticism. "I don't trust UConn at all" (Brennan). Weakest 2 seed. Lost Big East final in blowout.
- Ranked 4th in non-conference but 11th in Big East play. Guards can't execute their complex offensive system.
- **Consensus**: 7/9 sources bearish. Multiple pick them losing to MSU or UCLA in Sweet 16. Our model: 1.5%.
- **Basis**: Mixed data + vibes

### Key Mid-Seeds

#### St. John's (5 seed, East) — BULLISH / UNDERSEEDED

- "Most underseeded team in the tournament" (Evan Miya, repeated in 3 episodes). Quality of a 3 seed. Won Big East tournament. Zubie Edji is a matchup nightmare.
- **Bear case** (bur_2): Half-court offense not a strength. Northern Iowa could slow them down. Brennan actually picks the UNI upset.
- **Consensus**: Strong majority bullish. Multiple sources pick them out of the East over Duke. Our model: 0.6% -- may be undervaluing.
- **Basis**: Mixed -- data says underseeded, vibes-based distrust from some

#### Vanderbilt (5 seed, South) — BULLISH / UNDERSEEDED

- Beat Florida by 17. "Closer to a 3 than a 5." Best chance of making Elite 8 outside top-3 seeds per simulations.
- Tyler Tanner and Deuce Miles one of the best guard duos. Familiarity with Florida could help in potential Sweet 16 rematch.
- **Consensus**: Universal respect. Evan Miya picks them over Florida. Our model: 0.9%.
- **Basis**: Data-driven

#### Illinois (3 seed, South) — BULLISH WITH CEILING CONCERNS

- "Least discussed genuine national tournament threat" (Ralph). Best in tournament at going on kill shots. #1 offense. Massive bracket value (38% model vs 21% public for Elite 8).
- **Concern**: Defense has cracks. Can't create turnovers. Recent stagnation. "There's a limiter" (Brennan). Not physical enough.
- **Consensus**: Most pick Sweet 16+. Evan Miya picks Final Four. But Houston may be a wall. Our model: 2.3%.
- **Basis**: Data-driven (vibes create the ceiling concern)

#### Wisconsin (5 seed, West) — UNIVERSAL SLEEPER

- Wins at Michigan, Illinois, Purdue. Boyd + Blackwell backcourt praised everywhere.
- Evan Miya: "Throw out the numbers, just vibes -- they've felt like an Elite 8 team for a month."
- Frazier picks them to upset Arizona in Sweet 16. Multiple sources flag as dangerous.
- **Concern**: Inconsistent -- could also lose to High Point. "Iowa 2022 vibes" (picked for E8, lost R1 to Richmond).
- **Consensus**: Universal sleeper pick. Our model: 0.2% -- may be significantly undervaluing.
- **Basis**: Vibes-heavy with data support (resume vs good teams)

#### Tennessee (6 seed, Midwest) — SHARP SPLIT

- **Bull case**: 46% offensive rebound rate (historically elite). Bully ball creates high floor. Underseeded per Evan Miya. Trotter picks them for Elite 8.
- **Bear case**: "Worst shot-making team I've ever seen outside of Jacobe Gillespie" (Winnick). Both bur_1 hosts pick against them.
- **Consensus**: Sharp split. Our model: 0.1%.
- **Basis**: Data on both sides

#### Alabama (4 seed, Midwest) — BEARISH

- Aiden Holloway arrested (felony) -- off the team. Without him: offensive efficiency drops 12 pts/100, 3PT% falls from 38% to 33%.
- Worst defense on 1-6 seed lines. Gets outrebounded. High-variance 3PT dependent.
- **Consensus**: Universal concern. Multiple sources pick Hofstra upset. Our model: 0.5%.
- **Basis**: Data-driven

#### North Carolina (6/7 seed, South) — VERY BEARISH

- Universal fade. Caleb Wilson out for season. 350th in momentum, 364th away from home. Guard play "poor."
- "I think Illinois would score 115 on Carolina" (Frazier).
- **Consensus**: 7/9 sources pick VCU to beat them. Our model: 0.1%.
- **Basis**: Data-driven

#### Kentucky (7/10 seed, Midwest) — BEARISH

- "Hot garbage for most of the year" (Brennan). 355th in away-from-home metrics. 43rd vs top-100.
- **Consensus**: Near-universal pick for Santa Clara upset. Our model: 0.1%.
- **Basis**: Data-driven

#### UCLA (7 seed, East) — BULLISH IF HEALTHY

- Donovan Dent elite last 6-7 games (78 assists, 6 turnovers in one stretch). Ranked 8th since Feb 18.
- Injury concerns (Dent, Bilodeau) manageable per most sources. Multiple flag UCLA-over-UConn R32 upset.
- **Consensus**: Most bullish. Our model: 0.0% -- likely undervaluing.
- **Basis**: Data-driven (recent form metrics)

#### Gonzaga (3 seed, West) — QUIETLY POSITIVE

- Playing in Portland (home region). Wing length improved. Undertalked.
- Braden Huff likely out -- limits ceiling. With Huff would be title contender.
- Frazier's "shock" Final Four pick. Most others have them Sweet 16 ceiling.
- **Basis**: Vibes + matchup analysis

#### Virginia (3 seed, Midwest) — SNEAKY POSITIVE

- Ryan Odom praised as coach of the year candidate. Almost beat Duke in ACC title game.
- Big front court matches up with Michigan's bigs. Multiple sources have Sweet 16.
- **Concern**: Haven't beaten a 1 or 2 seed. Our model: 1.0%.
- **Basis**: Mixed

#### Arkansas (4 seed, West) — BULLISH

- Won SEC tournament. Darius Akoff averaging 30+ and 7+ assists. Elite isolation team.
- **Concern** (bur_1 Ralph): Calipari teams "don't practice against zones." Hawaii's no-help defense similar to Florida's scheme that gave Akoff trouble.
- **Basis**: Mixed -- star power vibes + scheme analysis

#### Texas Tech (5 seed, Midwest) — MODERATE POSITIVE

- Grant McCasland praised everywhere. Ranked 14th at Torvik since Toppin injury. Still strong despite missing key player.
- With Toppin healthy would be a 3 seed and Final Four contender.
- **Basis**: Data + coaching quality
